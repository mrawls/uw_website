Meredith L. Rawls, PhD

Developing software for a firehose of telescope data and using binary stars to study the cosmos

I am a postdoc working with the Large Synoptic Survey Telescope (LSST) Data Management (DM) group at the University of Washington. The LSST will begin its ten-year mission to continually image the sky in 2022, so we need software ready to handle terabytes of nightly data well before then. I'm also an active researcher in stellar astrophysics and a relentless science communicator.
